2008 Albums: Individual Writer's Lists

By No Ripcord Staff

As promised earlier in the week, here are the 2008 album lists from our individual writers. The majority of these were used to compile our overall list, however it is worth noting that a few were submitted (or changed) after the deadline, so those trying to work out the validity of our final 50 will struggle to make these fit!

A lot of people have commented on how the selections in the higher reaches of our list - particularly #50 to #26 - were arguably more interesting than the top 25, which tended to focus on the more widely acknowledged albums of 2008. I think our writers' individual lists possess a similar charm; there are some unusual and unexpected picks here, and collectively they provide an intriguing insight into how we all saw 2008 as music fans.
