Top 50 Albums of 2011 (Individual Writers' Lists)

By No Ripcord Staff
Now you've digested our Top 50, here's the final word on what each of our writers voted for. If you're statistically-minded, you might like to partake in a little light analysis. For instance:
  • Despite placing in our Top 5, no writer placed St. Vincent's Strange Mercy in their Top 3 records of the year.
  • Katy B's On A Mission - which placed inside the Top 10 - received the same number of votes as TV On The Radio's Nine Types of Light, which didn't make the Top 50 at all.
Any more interesting facts and figures, or just good, old-fashioned differences of opinion, can be shared and debated using the Disqus form below.
