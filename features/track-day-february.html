Track Of The Day: February

By Joe Iliff
“TOTD: like Match of the day, but featuring much less football and more handpicked songs from the web's finest blogs.“

February was a month that, like many other months, came and went. It’s held a few fun surprises: Gaddafi received an Oscar or something (current affairs is not my strong point); Arsenal failed to win a trophy… again; and Arcade Fire repeatedly kicked the pop industry in the teeth – sorry, no, that’s just a metaphor for winning awards – I know, I would’ve liked to have seen Simon Cowell’s bloodied face too.

But in the meantime we’ve been serving up some choice cuts for your indie delectation. I’d like to make a tip of the hat to Joe Rivers and David Coleman for making some great suggestions as well as making them there fancy hyperlink thingamajigs to aid your listening pleasure.

If you can’t get enough there’s a co-existing TOTD feature on our Facebook page – or in case you think my selections are, put politely, not very good.

These songs aren’t the biggest songs of the month; they’re not an ‘Indie top 40’ (because we figure that you’ve already heard all of those): but they are rough diamonds that have wormed their ways into our consciousness over the past thirty odd days. So here’s February’s list – I suggest you pick a few at random, listen to them, and feel free to make some comments… after all TOTD is really a benign dictatorship:
