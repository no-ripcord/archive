About Me

Joe's love of music started at the beginning of the new millennium when he saw U2's music video for "Beautiful Day" at the young age of 12. From there, he discovered Radiohead, David Bowie, Nine Inch Nails, St. Vincent and everything in-between. While "indie" is now a permanent part of his everyday vocabulary, he'll listen to any music that offers something new and exciting.
